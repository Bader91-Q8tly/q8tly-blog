MizuMesa is a Nikkei restaurant inside Al-Shaheed Tower in the KIPCO area of Kuwait City, pairing a Japanese cooking base with ingredients and flavours from Peru. The idea isn't just "Japanese with a twist" — there's a clear Peruvian hand in the dishes and the plating.

The place is upscale and works for lunch or dinner, with indoor seating, a family section, and room for groups. Beef dishes are among the menu's standouts, alongside Nikkei plates that blend sushi, sauces, and bold flavours.
