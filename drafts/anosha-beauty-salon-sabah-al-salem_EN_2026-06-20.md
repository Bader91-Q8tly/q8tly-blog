# Anosha Beauty Salon, Sabah Al-Salem: A Calm, By-Appointment Escape

> **Editorial of the week** — a first-hand look at one Sabah Al-Salem salon that gets the small things right.

Some salons sell the buzz — the crowd at the door, the music up, the sense that something is always happening. **Anosha Beauty Salon** sells the opposite, and does it well. Tucked on the third floor in **Sabah Al-Salem**, this **women-only** salon runs entirely **by appointment**, and that one decision quietly shapes everything else: no waiting around, no crowd, no rush. You book a time, you arrive, and the place is yours.

It is, simply, calm — and spotless.

## What stood out

The first thing you notice is how *uncrowded* it feels. Because every visit is booked, the salon never tips into the commotion you learn to tolerate elsewhere. The music stays low — the kind that actually lets you switch off rather than the kind you have to talk over.

The space itself is genuinely refined. The detailing is considered, not just clean: warm wood, soft lighting, a wall of nail colours arranged like a paint box, and a row of reclining wash chairs that make the basin step feel like part of the treatment rather than a chore.

What our visit kept coming back to, though, wasn't the polish — it was the **attention**. Staff checking in on how comfortable you were, asking what you actually needed, with a warmth in the hospitality that you don't always get. You're offered a coffee and water when you sit down, and it sets the tone for the whole appointment.

## A small thing worth mentioning

There's no dedicated prayer room, but when asked, the staff went out of their way: they set up a private, screened space and provided a prayer thobe, a mat, and marked the qibla direction. It's the kind of accommodate-on-request hospitality that tells you how a place treats its guests — offered without fuss.

## Who it's for

Anosha is for people who would rather not wait — walk in to your booked slot, get what you came for, and go — and for anyone who prizes a quiet, unhurried vibe over busy-salon energy. It's an easy pick for a relaxed everyday appointment, and it scales up to graduations, bridal showers and full bridal days when you want the occasion handled in one calm place.

## Frequently asked questions

**Where is Anosha Beauty Salon?**
In Sabah Al-Salem (Mubarak Al-Kabeer governorate), on the third floor, with easy parking behind the building.

**Do I need an appointment?**
Yes — Anosha runs by appointment, which is what keeps it calm and uncrowded. Call [+965 6566 5028](tel:+96565665028) or message [@anosha_salon](https://www.instagram.com/anosha_salon/) to book.

**Is Anosha women-only?**
Yes, it's a women-only salon.

**What are the opening hours?**
Daily, 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, including Friday.

**How much is a manicure or blow-dry?**
Manicures start from about 6 KD and a regular blow-dry from about 6 KD. Prices are indicative — confirm when you book.

**Does Anosha do bridal?**
Yes — from a bridal hairstyle to a full VIP Bridal Package, plus VIP room packages for birthdays, graduations and bridal showers.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
KNET and Visa / Mastercard.
